This is Dominic

Game Engineer

Hi, I'm Dominic Rubin, a twenty-one year old game design major studying at Fitchburg State University with an immense interest in the functionality of games. Ever since my introduction to gaming as a kid, I've pondered why and how they work the way they do. Jump forward sixteen years, and this interest has developed into a full-blown sense of determination to not only understand how games work, but be a part of the creation myself.
